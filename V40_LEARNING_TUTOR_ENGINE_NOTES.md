## Purpose
A quest should not only say what to type. It should teach:

- what to build
- why it matters
- the real-world analogy
- the mental model
- exact steps
- expected results
- proof requirements
- common mistakes
- reflection questions

## Rule
Do not just copy. Explain it back.
