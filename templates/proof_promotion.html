How to use this

Review proof, then promote

  • Open the verification quest.
  • Confirm the uploaded code, screenshot, or written answer proves the skill.
  • Promote only after the proof is reviewable.
  • KDT OS writes the result to skill_library/skills.json and reports/verified_skill_ledger.json.

Why it matters

Estimated is not trusted

Learning Intelligence can estimate that you have seen a skill, but V34 is what lets KDT OS remember that you proved it.
